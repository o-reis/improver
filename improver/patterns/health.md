# Learned Patterns - Health

Patterns specific to fitness and wellness.

## Fitness Patterns

## Pattern: Consistency Over Intensity

**Trigger:** Going too hard too soon  
**Principle:** Sustainable beats spectacular  
**Action:** Start at 50% effort, build up

## Pattern: Progressive Overload

**Trigger:** Plateau in progress  
**Principle:** Adaptation requires increasing demand  
**Action:** Add weight, reps, or difficulty gradually

## Pattern: Rest Is Part of Training

**Trigger:** Overtraining or burnout  
**Principle:** Recovery is when adaptation happens  
**Action:** Schedule rest days intentionally

## Nutrition Patterns

## Pattern: Protein Priority

**Trigger:** Not seeing muscle gains  
**Principle:** Protein builds muscle, not carbs or fat  
**Action:** Prioritize protein at each meal (0.8-1g/lb bodyweight)

## Pattern: Habit Stacking

**Trigger:** Forgetting to eat well  
**Principle:** Attach new habits to existing ones  
**Action:** After I [existing habit], I will [new habit]

## Pattern: 80/20 Rule

**Trigger:** Perfectionism leading to failure  
**Principle:** Good enough most of the time works  
**Action:** 80% adherence, 20% flexibility

## Mental Wellness Patterns

## Pattern: Stress First

**Trigger:** Physical goals stalled  
**Principle:** Stress impairs all other goals  
**Action:** Address stress before adding training

## Pattern: Sleep Priority

**Trigger:** Low energy, slow progress  
**Principle:** Sleep is when you actually change  
**Action:** Protect sleep like a training session

## Pattern: Small Wins

**Trigger:** Feeling overwhelmed  
**Principle:** Small actions build identity  
**Action:** One small healthy choice daily

## Recovery Patterns

## Pattern: Movement as Medicine

**Trigger:** Low back pain, stiffness  
**Principle:** Motion is lotion  
**Action:** Move frequently, gently

## Pattern: Contrast Therapy

**Trigger:** Muscle soreness  
**Principle:** Blood flow accelerates recovery  
**Action:** Hot/cold or activity breaks

## Pattern: Gradual Change

**Trigger:** Attempting total transformation  
**Principle:** Body resists sudden change  
**Action:** Change one thing at a time

## Reference Information

### Training Fundamentals
- Beginners: 3x/week
- Intermediate: 4-5x/week
- Compound movements first
- Deload periodically

### DOMS vs Injury
- DOMS: normal, fades
- Sharp pain: stop
- Persistent pain: see professional

### When to Rest
- Elevated resting heart rate
- Persistent fatigue
- Mood changes
- Performance drop
