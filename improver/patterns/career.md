# Learned Patterns - Career

Patterns specific to professional development.

## Job Search Patterns

## Pattern: Quality Applications

**Principle:** 100 mediocre applications < 10 excellent ones  
**Action:** Research deeply, customize each application

## Pattern: Referral Generation

**Principle:** Referrals skip the ATS and first screen  
**Action:** Ask for referrals from every connection monthly

## Pattern: Value First

**Principle:** Networking isn't asking, it's offering  
**Action:** What can you share or help with first?

## Growth Patterns

## Pattern: Visible Wins

**Principle:** Invisibility means no promotion  
**Action:** Document and communicate achievements monthly

## Pattern: Sponsor Seeking

**Principle:** Mentors advise, sponsors advocate  
**Action:** Find someone who will vouch for you

## Pattern: Skill Breadth vs. Depth

**Principle:** Early career needs depth; mid-career needs breadth  
**Action:** Know which you need now

## Negotiation Patterns

## Pattern: Always Negotiate

**Principle:** Most first offers have 10-15% buffer  
**Action:** Always counter, professionally

## Pattern: Total Comp View

**Principle:** Focus on full package, not just salary  
**Action:** Evaluate base, bonus, equity, benefits together

## Pattern: Walkaway Preparation

**Principle:** Best negotiation position is willingness to walk  
**Action:** Know your minimum before negotiating
