# Learned Patterns - Base

Universal patterns applicable across domains.

## Goal Setting

## Pattern: SMART Goals

**Principle:** Vague goals produce vague results  
**Action:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound

## Pattern: Minimum Viable Progress

**Principle:** Small wins build momentum  
**Action:** What is the smallest step that counts as progress?

## Pattern: Constraint-Driven Creativity

**Principle:** Unlimited options paralyze; constraints focus  
**Action:** Define constraints first, then solve within them

## Execution Patterns

## Pattern: Two-Hour Blocks

**Principle:** Deep work requires uninterrupted time  
**Action:** Protect 2-hour blocks for important work

## Pattern: Weekly Review

**Principle:** Without reflection, experience teaches nothing  
**Action:** 30-minute weekly review of what worked/failed

## Pattern: Default to Action

**Principle:** Analysis paralysis is the enemy  
**Action:** When stuck, take one imperfect action

## Decision Patterns

## Pattern: Reversibility Test

**Principle:** Reversible decisions can be made quickly  
**Action:** Can this be undone? If yes, decide fast

## Pattern: Regret Minimization

**Principle:** Long-term regret outweighs short-term comfort  
**Action:** What will you regret not doing?

## Pattern: 10/10/10 Rule

**Principle:** emotions cloud judgment  
**Action:** How will you feel in 10 minutes? 10 months? 10 years?
