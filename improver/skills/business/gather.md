# Business Skill - Gathering Questions

## Business Stage

### Idea Stage
- What problem are you solving?
- Who experiences this problem?
- How do you know this problem exists?
- What is your solution?
- Why are you the one to solve this?

### MVP Stage
- Do you have a working product?
- Who are your early users?
- What feedback have you received?
- Have you validated willingness to pay?
- What is your development roadmap?

### Growth Stage
- What is your current revenue?
- What is your growth rate?
- What is your unit economics?
- What is limiting growth?
- What resources do you need to scale?

### Scale Stage
- What systems need to be built?
- Are you profitable or what is the path to profitability?
- What is your competitive moat?
- Is leadership/team ready to scale?
- What are the risks of scaling?

## Business Model

### Value Proposition
- What specific value do you deliver?
- How is this different from alternatives?
- What is your unique advantage?

### Customer Segments
- Who are your target customers?
- Have you spoken to real customers?
- What segments are most valuable?
- Where do they spend time?

### Revenue Model
- How do you make money?
- What price do you charge?
- What is customer lifetime value?
- What is your gross margin?

### Channels
- How do you reach customers?
- What is your CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)?
- Which channels work best?
- What is your sales process?

## Resources & Constraints

### Team
- Who is on your team?
- What skills do you have?
- What skills are you missing?
- Can you afford to hire?

### Capital
- How much money do you have?
- What are your monthly expenses?
- How long can you run?
- Are you seeking external funding?

### Time
- How much time can you dedicate?
- Is this full-time or side project?
- What are your personal constraints?
