# Business Skill - Research Framework

## Market Research

### TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis
- **TAM:** Total market size
- **SAM:** Serviceable addressable market
- **SOM:** Serviceable obtainable market

**Queries:**
- "{industry} market size"
- "{industry} market growth rate"
- "{industry} trends {current_year}"

### Competitive Landscape
- Who are the main players?
- What are their strengths/weaknesses?
- What is the competitive moat opportunity?
- How is the market fragmented?

**Queries:**
- "{industry} competitors"
- "{competitor} business model"
- "{industry} market share"

## Business Model Research

### Viability Questions
- Does this business model work at scale?
- What are the unit economics?
- What is the typical path to profitability?

**Queries:**
- "{business_model} profitability"
- "{business_model} unit economics"
- "saas metrics benchmarks"

### Customer Acquisition
- What channels work in this market?
- What is typical CAC?
- What conversion rates to expect?

**Queries:**
- "{industry} customer acquisition cost"
- "{channel} conversion rates"
- "{industry} marketing strategies"

## Funding Research

### Funding Landscape
- What funding is available for this stage?
- What do investors in this space look for?
- What are typical valuations?

**Queries:**
- "{industry} funding trends {current_year}"
- "{stage} startup valuation"
- "investor preferences {industry}"

### Pitch & Traction
- What metrics matter most?
- What traction indicators impress investors?
- How to structure a pitch?
