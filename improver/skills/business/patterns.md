# Business Skill - Patterns

## Validation Patterns

## Pattern: Continuous Validation

**Trigger:** Building without customer input  
**Principle:** Assumptions without validation are expensive  
**Action:** Talk to 10 customers before building more

## Pattern: MVP First

**Trigger:** Building full product upfront  
**Principle:** Validate demand before investing  
**Action:** What is the minimum to test this idea?

## Pattern: Paid Over Free

**Trigger:** Offering free to get users  
**Principle:** Paid customers validate value  
**Action:** Try charging from day one

## Growth Patterns

## Pattern: Concentrated Channels

**Principle:** Spreading thin across channels wastes resources  
**Action:** Find 1-2 channels that work and double down

## Pattern: Retention Before Acquisition

**Principle:** Acquiring users who churn wastes money  
**Action:** Fix retention before scaling acquisition

## Pattern: Cohort Analysis

**Principle:** Aggregate metrics hide truth  
**Action:** Analyze by cohort to find patterns

## Strategy Patterns

## Pattern: Narrow Focus

**Principle:** Startups die from distraction  
**Action:** What is the one thing that matters?

## Pattern: Build Moat Incrementally

**Principle:** Early advantages compound  
**Action:** What defensibility can you build now?

## Pattern: Strategic Patience

**Principle:** Right timing beats fast execution  
**Action:** Is the market ready? Is the team ready?

## Funding Patterns

## Pattern: Runway Awareness

**Principle:** Dead startups can't pivot  
**Action:** Always know your months of runway

## Pattern: Raise Before You Need

**Principle:** Fundraising when desperate shows  
**Action:** Raise when you have options

## Pattern: Milestone-Based Raises

**Principle:** Investors buy progress  
**Action:** Raise to next milestone, not for burn
