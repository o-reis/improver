# Tech Skill - Gathering Questions

## Technical Background

### Current Level
- What is your experience level? (junior, mid, senior)
- How long have you been coding?
- What languages/frameworks do you know well?
- What have you built before?

### Current Role
- Are you learning, working, or leading?
- What is your typical day like?
- What challenges do you face?

## Project Context

### Problem Definition
- What problem are you solving?
- Who experiences this problem?
- How is it solved today?
- What would an ideal solution look like?

### Technical Scope
- What is the project type? (web, mobile, desktop, CLI, API)
- What is the expected scale? (users, data volume)
- Are there real-time requirements?
- What integrations are needed?

### Stack Decisions
- Do you have technology constraints?
- What is your team experienced in?
- What are the performance requirements?
- Are there security considerations?

## Constraints & Resources

### Timeline
- When do you need results?
- What are the milestones?
- Are there hard deadlines?

### Team
- Who is working on this?
- What are their skills?
- How will you coordinate?

### Budget
- What infrastructure budget exists?
- Can you use paid services?
- Do you need open-source only?
