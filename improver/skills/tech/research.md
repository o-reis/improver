# Tech Skill - Research Framework

## Technology Research

### Stack Selection

**Questions:**
- What does the community say?
- What is the job market for this stack?
- What is the long-term viability?
- How steep is the learning curve?

**Queries:**
- "{stack} vs {alternative} comparison {current_year}"
- "{language} job market trends"
- "{framework} community size"
- "{technology} enterprise adoption"

### Best Practices

**Queries:**
- "{language} best practices"
- "{architecture} patterns"
- "clean code {language}"
- "{framework} security best practices"

### Performance

**Queries:**
- "{technology} performance benchmark"
- "{database} vs {alternative} speed"
- "{language} scalability"
