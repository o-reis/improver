# Education Skill - Gathering Questions

## Learning Goal

### What & Why
- What exactly do you want to learn?
- Why is this important to you?
- How will you use this knowledge?
- What does mastery look like?

### Scope
- Broad overview or deep expertise?
- How much detail is needed?
- Are there specific topics to prioritize?
- What can be skipped?

### Timeline
- When do you need to know this?
- Is there an exam or deadline?
- How flexible is your timeline?
- What milestones exist?

## Current State

### Prior Knowledge
- What do you already know?
- What related skills do you have?
- Have you attempted this before?
- What was your experience?

### Resources Available
- What time do you have?
- What budget exists?
- What access do you have? (courses, books, mentors)
- What environment will you learn in?

### Constraints
- Other responsibilities?
- Learning disabilities or challenges?
- Specific requirements? (certification, accreditation)
- Distance or remote considerations?

## Learning Preferences

### Style & Method
- Do you prefer visual, auditory, or hands-on?
- Do you like structure or flexibility?
- Do you learn better alone or in groups?
- Do you prefer reading, videos, or interactive?

### Motivation
- What keeps you motivated?
- What typically derails you?
- Do you have accountability?
- How do you track progress?
