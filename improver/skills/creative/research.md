# Creative Skill - Research Framework

## Industry Research

### For Musicians

**Key Areas:**
- Streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube)
- Live performance ecosystem
- Sync licensing opportunities
- Independent vs. label dynamics
- Music discovery algorithms

**Research Queries:**
- "independent musician success statistics {current_year}"
- "Spotify algorithm for artist discovery"
- "how independent artists make money"
- "music industry gatekeepers changing"
- "successful unsigned artists case studies"

### For Visual Artists

**Key Areas:**
- Gallery vs. independent sales
- NFT and digital art market
- Social media for artists (Instagram, Behance, ArtStation)
- Commission work strategies
- Art licensing

**Research Queries:**
- "how artists build collector base"
- "art galleries accepting new artists"
- "successful self-taught artists"
- "art licensing income"
- "social media for visual artists"

### For Writers

**Key Areas:**
- Traditional publishing vs. self-publishing
- Genre-specific markets
- Platform writing (Medium, Substack)
- Adaptation potential
- Writing communities and critique groups

**Research Queries:**
- "debut author success rates"
- "self-publishing vs traditional publishing"
- "successful writers without agents"
- "platform building for writers"

## Comparative Analysis

Create comparison table for creative success:

| Factor | Common in Success | Rare in Success |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| Consistency | Daily/weekly output | Sporadic bursts |
| Community | Active engagement | Isolated creation |
| Business skills | Developing them | Ignoring them |
| Adaptability | Platform pivoting | Rigid approach |

## Synthesis Questions

1. What patterns emerge across successful creatives in this field?
2. What barriers exist and how do successful people overcome them?
3. What is the current opportunity landscape?
4. What is the realistic timeline for different paths?
