# Creative Skill - Gathering Questions

## Artistic Foundation

### Discipline & Identity
- What is your primary art form? (music, visual art, writing, performance)
- How long have you been practicing?
- What is your stylistic focus?
- Do you create solo or collaborate?

### Current Position
- Are you unsigned/independent, signed, or seeking representation?
- Do you have releases, exhibitions, or publications?
- What has been your biggest achievement so far?
- What does "success" mean to you in your field?

### Resources & Obstacles
- What equipment, space, or tools do you have access to?
- What is your financial situation for investing in your craft?
- What is your time availability?
- What skills besides your art do you have? (marketing, business, tech)

## Industry Context

### Market Understanding
- Who is your target audience?
- Which platforms do you use or want to use?
- Are there industry gatekeepers relevant to your path?
- Do you have mentors or connections in the industry?

### Distribution & Discovery
- How do you currently reach audiences?
- Have you tried specific discovery strategies?
- What feedback have you received?
- Is location important for your art? (scene, market access)

## Psychological

### Motivation & Persistence
- What keeps you creating despite setbacks?
- How do you handle rejection or stagnation?
- Are you creating for yourself or for an audience?
- What would make you feel fulfilled?

### Pressure Points
- Are you facing external pressure to "succeed"?
- Do you compare yourself to others?
- What are you most afraid of?
