# Creative Skill - Patterns

Domain-specific patterns for creative careers.

## Pattern: Consistency Over Perfection

**Trigger:** Artist produces rarely due to perfectionism  
**Principle:** Volume creates quality through iteration  
**Action:** Ship something imperfect weekly. Quality follows output.

## Pattern: Audience First

**Trigger:** Artist creates in isolation  
**Principle:** Art without audience is hobby, not career  
**Action:** Who is one person who would care about this? Start there.

## Pattern: Discovery > Creation

**Trigger:** Artist creates but doesn't distribute  
**Principle:** Unseen art has zero career impact  
**Action:** What is the minimum distribution? One platform, one post.

## Pattern: Skill Stacking

**Trigger:** Artist focuses only on craft  
**Principle:** Business skills multiply art impact  
**Action:** What one business skill would most help your art?

## Pattern: Scene Strategy

**Trigger:** Artist ignores geographic community  
**Principle:** Local scene provides early support and feedback  
**Action:** What is the local community for your art?

## Pattern: Gatekeeper Bypass

**Trigger:** Artist waits for industry validation  
**Principle:** Direct-to-fan models bypass traditional gatekeepers  
**Action:** What if you had 100 true fans? How would you reach them?

## Pattern: Collaboration Currency

**Trigger:** Artist works alone  
**Principle:** Collaboration expands audience and skill  
**Action:** Who could you create with this week?

## Pattern: Revenue Diversification

**Trigger:** Artist relies on single income source  
**Principle:** Multiple streams provide stability  
**Action:** List 5 ways successful artists make money. Which apply to you?

## Pattern: Public Journey

**Trigger:** Artist hides work until "ready"  
**Principle:** Building audience takes time, start now  
**Action:** Document your process. Others will follow the journey.

## Pattern: Niche Authority

**Trigger:** Artist tries to appeal to everyone  
**Principle:** Depth in niche beats breadth in mass market  
**Action:** Who is the most devoted fan you could have? Appeal only to them.
