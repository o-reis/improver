# Career Skill - Patterns

## Pattern: Skill Gap Mapping

**Trigger:** Career stalling  
**Principle:** Identify the specific gap, not vague "more skills"  
**Approach:** "What does [successful person] do that you don't? Learn that."

## Pattern: Network First

**Trigger:** Job search without connections  
**Principle:** Most jobs come through networks, not applications  
**Approach:** "Make one meaningful connection this week, not 50 applications."

## Pattern: Transferable Wins

**Trigger:** Career change feels like starting over  
**Principle:** Skills transfer, just reframe them  
**Approach:** "How does [current skill] apply to [target role]?"

## Pattern: Side Door

**Trigger:** Direct path blocked  
**Principle:** Lateral moves often lead to desired destination  
**Approach:** "What adjacent role gets you closer?"

## Pattern: Reputation Building

**Trigger:** Invisible in current role  
**Principle:** Visibility compounds over time  
**Approach:** "What can you do to be known for one thing?"

## Pattern: Promotion Leverage

**Trigger:** Passed over for promotion  
**Principle:** Performance doesn't equal promotion  
**Approach:** "What do promotion decisions actually reward?"

## Pattern: Exit Interview Insight

**Trigger:** Considering leaving  
**Principle:** Most issues can be negotiated  
**Approach:** "Have you asked for what you need before leaving?"

## Pattern: Salary Negotiation

**Trigger:** Accepting first offer  
**Principle:** First offer is rarely best, rarely final  
**Approach:** "What is your walkaway number and their range?"

## Pattern: Portfolio Career

**Trigger:** Rigid job structure limiting  
**Principle:** Multiple income streams provide freedom  
**Approach:** "What skills could you monetize separately?"

## Pattern: 90-Day Proof

**Trigger:** New role or promotion anxiety  
**Principle:** First 90 days set trajectory  
**Approach:** "What would you need to achieve to exceed expectations?"
