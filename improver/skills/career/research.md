# Career Skill - Research Framework

## Research Categories

### Market Research

**Questions to Answer:**
- Is there demand for this role/transition?
- What is the job market like?
- What are growth projections?
- What is the competition like?

**Sample Queries:**
- "jobs in demand 2024 2025"
- "{industry} hiring trends"
- "{role} job market"
- "career transitions trending"

### Skill Research

**Questions to Answer:**
- What skills do target roles require?
- How can current skills transfer?
- What is the learning curve?
- What credentials matter?

**Sample Queries:**
- "skills for {role}"
- "{role} requirements"
- "transferable skills from {current_role}"
- "best certifications for {field}"

### Compensation Research

**Questions to Answer:**
- What is the salary range for target roles?
- How does it compare to current compensation?
- What benefits and perks are standard?
- How does it vary by location/company size?

**Sample Queries:**
- "average {role} salary"
- "{role} salary by experience"
- "{industry} compensation trends"
- "remote {role} salary"

### Path Research

**Questions to Answer:**
- How did others make this transition?
- What shortcuts or accelerators exist?
- What are common mistakes?
- What is realistic timeline?

**Sample Queries:**
- "success stories {transition_type}"
- "how to transition to {field}"
- "{role} career path examples"
- "day in life {role}"

## Interview Synthesis

Create a profile of successful career transitions:

| Element | Most Common Pattern |
|---------|-------------------|
| Time to transition | 6-18 months |
| Key factor | Network connections |
| Biggest barrier | Skill gaps |
| Common mistake | Not starting early enough |
| Accelerators | Certifications + projects |

## Actionable Output

1. **Top 3 Skills to Develop** - Ranked by impact
2. **Target Companies** - 5-10 realistic options
3. **Timeline** - Milestones for next 6-12 months
4. **Resources** - Courses, communities, tools
