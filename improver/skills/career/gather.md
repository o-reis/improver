# Career Skill - Gathering Questions

## Current State

### Role & Industry
- What is your current job title and industry?
- How long have you been in this role?
- What does a typical day look like?
- What are your primary responsibilities?
- What is your compensation range? (optional)

### Tenure & Trajectory
- How long have you been with your current employer?
- Have you been promoted or received raises?
- What is the typical career path in your field?
- What growth opportunities exist at your company?

### Motivation
- What specifically prompted this reflection now?
- What are you hoping to achieve?
- What would make you feel fulfilled in your career?
- What do you enjoy most/least about your current role?

## Target State

### Ideal Role
- What is your dream role or title?
- What industry or company type interests you?
- What level of responsibility do you want?
- Are you looking locally, remotely, or willing to relocate?

### Requirements
- What salary range do you need?
- What work-life balance is essential?
- What company culture matters to you?
- What non-negotiables do you have?

### Gap Analysis
- What skills do you have that transfer?
- What skills do you need to develop?
- What experience is missing?
- What barriers exist? (credentials, location, timing)

## Effort Investment

### Time & Resources
- How much time can you dedicate weekly to career development?
- What budget do you have for courses, certifications?
- Can you take risks (startups, relocation)?
- Do you have family or other constraints?

### Network
- Who do you know in your target field?
- What professional associations exist?
- Do you have mentors or advisors?
- What platforms are you active on?
