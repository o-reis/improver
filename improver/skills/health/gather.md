# Health Skill - Gathering Questions

## Current Health Status

### Physical Health
- What is your current fitness level?
- Do you have any injuries or conditions?
- When was your last physical?
- Do you take any medications?
- What is your general energy level?

### Health Goals
- What specifically do you want to achieve?
- Why is this important to you?
- What does success look like?
- What is your timeline?

### Past Attempts
- Have you tried this before?
- What worked? What didn't?
- Why did previous attempts fail?
- What did you learn?

## Lifestyle Context

### Time & Schedule
- How much time can you dedicate daily/weekly?
- What times of day work best?
- Are you consistent with schedule?
- What typically derails your plans?

### Resources & Access
- Do you have gym access?
- Do you have equipment at home?
- What is your budget for fitness?
- Can you afford coaching or classes?

### Support System
- Do you have people who support your goals?
- Are there people who undermine you?
- Do you prefer solo or group activities?

## Diet & Nutrition

### Current Diet
- What do you typically eat?
- Do you track anything now?
- Any food restrictions or allergies?
- Do you cook at home or eat out?

### Dietary Goals
- What dietary approach interests you?
- Are you willing to meal prep?
- What are your food preferences?

## Mental & Recovery

### Sleep
- How much sleep do you get?
- What is your sleep quality?
- Do you have a sleep schedule?

### Stress
- What is your typical stress level?
- Do you have stress management practices?
- What triggers your stress?

### Recovery
- Do you stretch or foam roll?
- Do you take rest days?
- How do you handle soreness?
